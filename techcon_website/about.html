Our History

TechCon was founded in 2010 with the mission to connect technology enthusiasts and professionals from around the world. Over the years, it has grown into one of the most anticipated tech events, featuring keynote speakers, workshops, and networking opportunities.

Our conference has always been at the forefront of technological advancements, showcasing innovations in AI, blockchain, cybersecurity, and more. We are proud to have hosted industry leaders and visionaries who have shared their insights and experiences with our community.

Mission

Our mission is to bring together innovators, practitioners, and learners to share knowledge, foster collaboration, and accelerate the development of meaningful, ethical technology. We focus on practical talks, hands-on workshops, and creating opportunities for networking across industries.
